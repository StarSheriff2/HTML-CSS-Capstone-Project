SALSA AND BACHATA SUMMIT
2020

The Salsa and Bachata Summit 2020 is the biggest Salsa and Bachata event in the region. This September 18, 19 and 20 Tegucigalpa becomes the capital of salsa in Central America. Come and enjoy amateur and semi-professional competitions, male solos and more than 45 shows by national and foreign dance teams. This year, we hope to have a greater attendance as more dance schools from different parts of the region have already signed up for this event. All Salsa and Bachata lovers are invited to this summit and together share the love and passion for tropical dance and music.

Please contact us per email for any further questions about Salsa and Bachata Summit 2020!
event@salsanation.com.hn
