SALSA AND BACHATA SUMMIT
2020

An annual Salsa celebration, with top performers from all of latin america for one week of music and dance is taking place in December, in Honduras.

Event Date

2020.12.16(WED) ~ 20(SUN)

@ Salon Enrique X, Hotel Clarion

Main Program

Seminaries

Learn about the latest trends and innovations in tropical dance from around the globe.

Contests

See some of the world's top performers compete in different categories.

Workshops

Learn from the best with specialized workshops from world class instructors.

After Party

Free-pass to our 2 after parties where you'll get the chance to meet other dancers just like you.

Prizes

There will be many prizes and surprises for all attendees: tickets, coupons and many gifts.

Featured Instructors

William McKensey

Co-founder, owner and director for Azucar Dance Studio | Instructor | DJ

He's a world-renowned Salsa dancer, Certified Instructor and Instructor Trainer and holds a Master's Degree in Music Education.

Jean Pierre

Director at Mexico Kizomba

One of the foremost proponents of Kizomba dance and music in the world.

Fito Gonzalez

General Director at Salsa Style School

Fito is the biggest proponent of Salsa in Honduras and Central America. He holds a certificate by the Puerto Rico Salsa Open in Puerto Rican salsa and the folkloric ballet of the Dominican Republic.

Maria Zuniga

Artistic Director at Salsa Style School

One of the main dancers of the national Garifuna ballet for seven years. She is certified by the Santo Domingo ballet, from the Dominican Republic.

Diego Salsita

Director at Diego Productions | Project Bachateros Factory

Diego runs his own 3-story dance school in Bogota, Colombia. He is a four-time world bachata champion and also organizes one of the largest salsa congresses and competitions in his city.

Lupita Sierra

Director at NYC Salsa Studio

Lupita is a world-renown salsa dancer, who travels as an artist, choreographer and instructor while conquering hearts and stages across the globe.
